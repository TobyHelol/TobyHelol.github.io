About Me

My is Toby He. I'm a Grade 10 student at ISF. I come from Nanjing and have studied at Nanjing Foreign Language School (NFLS) for three years.

Chemistry has always fascinated me. The way molecules interact, the principles behind chemical reactions, and the endless possibilities for discovery have captivated my interest. I enjoy conducting experiments, exploring the periodic table, and understanding how chemistry impacts our daily lives.

Photography is another passion of mine. I love capturing moments and stories through the lens of a camera. Whether it's landscapes, portraits, or macro photography, I find joy in documenting the world around me and expressing my creativity through visual storytelling.

Animals hold a special place in my heart. I am fascinated by their diversity, behavior, and the important role they play in our ecosystem. I am an advocate for animal welfare and conservation, and I strive to raise awareness about the importance of protecting and preserving our natural world.

In addition to my academic pursuits and hobbies, I am also an avid badminton player. I enjoy the competitive nature of the sport and the physical and mental challenges it presents. Badminton allows me to stay active, improve my coordination, and develop valuable skills such as discipline and perseverance.

关于我

我的名字是何旻泽。我是弘立10年级金社的学生。我来自南京，初中就读于南京外国语学校。

化学 一直令我着迷。分子相互作用的方式、化学反应背后的原理以及无穷无尽的有机合成吸引着我的兴趣。我喜欢做实验，探索元素周期表，了解化学如何影响我们的日常生活。

摄影 是我的另一个爱好。我喜欢通过相机镜头捕捉瞬间和故事。无论是风光摄影、肖像摄影还是微距摄影，我都能从记录身边的世界中找到乐趣，并通过视觉故事来表达我的创造力。

我热爱环境，更喜欢可爱的动物 。它们的多样性、行为以及在生态系统中扮演的重要角色都让我着迷。我是动物福利和保护的倡导者。我努力提高人们对保护和维护自然世界重要性的认识。

除了学术追求和业余爱好，我还喜欢打羽毛球。 我喜欢这项运动的竞争性以及它带来的身体和心理挑战。羽毛球运动能让我保持活跃，提高协调能力，培养纪律性和毅力等宝贵技能。
